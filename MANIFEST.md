Manifest
========

`hunt.cpp`
  : A sample hunting algorithm (probably hunt00).

`hunt.hpp`
  : The header file that declares `huntDragon(World w)`

`hunt00.cpp`
  : Sample hunting algorithm, zero based, move backwards from end

`hunt01.cpp`
  : Sample hunting algorithm, one based, move backwards from end

`hunt02.cpp`
  : Sample hunting algorthm, zero based, always look in middle

`hunt03.cpp`
  : Sample hunting algorithm, zero based, jump forward 2 for burned
    farms and backwards 1 for ok villages

`test-hunt.cpp`
  : `main` procedure for running a bunch of tests.

`trace-hunt.cpp`
  : `main` procedure for tracing one situation.

`World.cpp` and `World.hpp`
  : Our world of farms. Look at `World.hpp` for details. Usage
    is also documented in the `README.md` file.
